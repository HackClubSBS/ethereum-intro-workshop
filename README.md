> 🗓 **Devcon 8** is happening at JIO World Center, Mumbai — **November 2026**. This workshop is the first step toward getting Ahmedabad students there.

---

## Objectives

- Give students their **first structured introduction to Ethereum** — regardless of technical background
- Host an **experienced Ethereum builder** to share real-world insights via a talk + AMA
- Create an **open networking space** for students interested in Web3
- Seed a **long-term Ethereum community** in Ahmedabad's student ecosystem
- Build pathways for students toward **Devcon 8, Mumbai**

---

## Event Agenda

> **Total Duration:** ~2.5 to 3 hours

| Duration | Session | Format | Details |
|----------|---------|--------|---------|
| 20 min | **Welcome & Introduction** | Presentation | HackClub SBS intro, overview of Devcon 8 Mumbai, why Ethereum matters |
| 30 min | **What is Ethereum?** | Lecture + Q&A | Blockchain basics, decentralisation, real-world relevance |
| 40 min | **Guest Speaker Session** | Talk + AMA | Experienced Ethereum developer shares their journey |
| 30 min | **Ethereum in the Real World** | Discussion | DeFi, DAOs, NFTs, Layer 2s — real use cases and career paths in Web3 |
| 20 min | **Career Paths in Web3** | Discussion | Opportunities for tech and business students in the Ethereum ecosystem |
| 20 min | **Q&A + Networking Meetup** | Open | Open discussion, Devcon ticket info, informal 1:1 networking |
