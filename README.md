# 🔷 Introduction to Ethereum — Campus Workshop & Networking Meetup
 
> **Organised by [HackClub SBS](https://github.com/HackClubSBS) · Shanti Business School, Ahmedabad**  
> Part of the [Ethereum Foundation's Road to Devcon 8 India University Program](https://esp.ethereum.foundation/applicants/rfp/rtd8_india_academic/apply)
 
---
 
## 📌 About This Event
 
This repository documents our proposal and planning for an **introductory Ethereum workshop and networking meetup** at Shanti Business School, Ahmedabad — open to all students, recent graduates, and curious minds across the city.
 
The event is being organised by **HackClub SBS**, a student-led technology club, as part of the Ethereum Foundation's **Road to Devcon 8 Academic Program**.
 
---
 
## 🎯 Objectives
 
- Give students their **first structured introduction to Ethereum** — regardless of technical background
- Host an **experienced Ethereum builder/speaker** to share real-world insights
- Create an **open networking space** for students interested in Web3
- Build a **long-term Ethereum community** in Ahmedabad's student ecosystem
- Create pathways for students toward **Devcon 8, Mumbai — November 2026**
---
 
## 🗓️ Event Agenda
 
| Duration | Session | Format |
|----------|---------|--------|
| 20 min | Welcome & Introduction | Presentation |
| 30 min | What is Ethereum? | Lecture + Q&A |
| 40 min | Guest Speaker Session | Talk + AMA |
| 30 min | Ethereum in the Real World | Discussion |
| 20 min | Career Paths in Web3 | Discussion |
| 20 min | Q&A + Networking Meetup | Open |
 
**Total Duration:** ~2.5 to 3 hours
 
---
 
## 👥 Target Audience
 
- Students from any college or university in Ahmedabad and nearby areas
- Recent graduates and working professionals curious about Ethereum
- No prior blockchain knowledge required — fully introductory
